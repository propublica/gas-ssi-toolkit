# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
- **User-facing documentation:** Friendly and approachable. The tone should be warm and less formal, making complex technical concepts accessible to a broader audience, including non-developers.
- **In-app text:** Concise and direct. Text within the application should be brief, to the point, and focused on immediate utility, providing essential information without extensive elaboration.

## Brand Messaging
The `gas-ssi-toolkit` should consistently convey a message of **Efficiency & Automation**. This emphasizes how the toolkit streamlines workflows, automates tasks, and helps users achieve their goals with greater productivity.
